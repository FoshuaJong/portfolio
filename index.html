01 / About

Turning complex constraints
into working systems

I'm an Operations Planning Engineer at Transpower New Zealand, the state-owned enterprise that owns and operates the national electricity transmission grid. I sit in the Outage Planning team, where I build the software, analytics, and optimisation systems behind how grid maintenance gets scheduled and assessed.

I hold a BE(Hons) First Class in Engineering Science from the University of Auckland, specialising in operations research — algorithmic optimisation, simulation, and data science. I was hired off Transpower's graduate programme a year ahead of schedule.

Outside of grid work, I'm interested in how optimisation and data tooling can be applied more broadly — from scheduling and logistics to analytics infrastructure and decision support.

Selected work

PROJECT 01

National Grid Outage Scheduler

A constraint-programming solver that generates the Annual Outage Plan for New Zealand's electricity transmission grid. Automates what was previously a multi-month manual planning process, optimising across hundreds of outages with complex interdependencies and operational constraints.

  • Reduced planning turnaround from four months to one
  • Improved schedule efficiency by 25% with zero conflicts
  • Models dependencies, seasonal windows, and crew availability
  • In production use for national grid planning
Python CP-SAT OR-Tools pandas
